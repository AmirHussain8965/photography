About me

Spaces tell stories —

my work is about translating those stories through
light, geometry, and perspective.

Who I Am

I’m Sokol Kokoshi, an architectural and commercial photographer based in Chapel Hill, North Carolina.

With more than 15 years of professional experience, I specialize in photographing architecture, interiors, and real estate projects across Europe and the United States. My work focuses on clarity, structure, and atmosphere — capturing how spaces feel as much as how they look.

Before moving to the U.S., I photographed architectural and commercial projects throughout Croatia, Montenegro, Albania, Greece, Northern Italy, Portugal, and Spain, and later continued working in New York, Washington DC, and Miami. I now collaborate with architects, designers, and builders across Raleigh–Durham, Charlotte, and the East Coast.

  • Based in Chapel Hill, North Carolina.
  • Member of ASMP & AIAP. Fully insured.
What I Do

I create architectural, interior, and real-estate photography that highlights form, structure, and atmosphere.
My images aim to express how spaces feel — not just how they look.

Each shoot begins with understanding the architect’s vision, studying the light, and finding the perspective that best tells the story of the space. Whether I’m photographing a large commercial development or a quiet residential project, I focus on balance, detail, and authenticity.
